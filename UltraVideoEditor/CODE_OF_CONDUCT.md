# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

Ultra Creative Suite is a project built by and for people with disabilities. We are committed to maintaining a welcoming, respectful, and inclusive community for everyone — regardless of disability status, age, experience level, nationality, or background.

---

## Our Standards

**We expect:**
- Respectful, constructive communication
- Patience with contributors of all experience levels
- Genuine consideration for accessibility needs
- Focus on what is best for the project and its users

**We do not tolerate:**
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Dismissive attitudes toward accessibility concerns
- Mockery of disability, assistive technology, or users who rely on them
- Spam, self-promotion unrelated to the project

---

## Accessibility as a Core Value

This project's primary users are blind and visually impaired people. Dismissing, minimizing, or deprioritizing accessibility concerns is a violation of this Code of Conduct.

All contributors are expected to approach accessibility with the same seriousness as correctness, performance, and security.

---

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please open an issue marked `conduct` or contact the project maintainer directly through GitHub.

All reports will be reviewed and handled with discretion.

---

## Enforcement

Violations may result in warnings, temporary restrictions, or permanent removal from the project, depending on severity and pattern.
